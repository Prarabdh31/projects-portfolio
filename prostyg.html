Project Stygian: RPG

Project Type: TPP Role Playing Game
Initial Deployment: July 2021
Latest Update: September 2021

Description

Project Stygian stands as a comprehensive 3rd Person Role-Playing Game (RPG) meticulously crafted within Unreal Engine 4 and subsequently ported to Unreal Engine 5, representing the culmination of my academic pursuits as part of my Final University Thesis. Serving as a pivotal component of my ongoing research paper, Stygian epitomizes a fully functional demo RPG, situated within the immersive realm of the Action-Adventure genre. Originally conceived and developed within Unreal Engine 4, Stygian seamlessly integrates characters sourced under Free Distribution Licenses from Epic Store, SketchFab, and Quixel Megascans, while animations are curated from reputable repositories such as Mixamo. The backend infrastructure of the game is engineered using Blueprint in Unreal Engine, complemented by robust local data storage facilitated by the B-tree algorithm. The intricate Game Logic and programming intricacies of Stygian are exclusively executed in C#, showcasing my proficiency in this versatile programming language. Marking a significant milestone in my career, Stygian represents my most ambitious undertaking to date, underscored by its expansive scale and immersive gameplay experience.
