Eidetic Mirror

Project Type: Grievance Cell
Initial Deployment: May 2022
Latest Update: July 2022

Description

Eidetic Mirror represents a pioneering project developed on Bubble.io, designed around the concept of "On this Day." This innovative Textboard Application serves to commemorate historical significance by showcasing events that transpired on the present date in previous years, underscoring the profound impact of each date throughout history. With a repository of global events spanning various epochs, Eidetic Mirror offers users the opportunity to explore historical milestones and trivia sorted by months. As the sole developer of this project, I take pride in its contribution to fostering an appreciation for history and providing access to intriguing historical insights that are often challenging to access.
